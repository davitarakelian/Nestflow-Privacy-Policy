PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: August 1, 2025

INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Nestflow, a multi-platform listing and connection application operated by Nestflow Corp ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Platform").

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information and how we will treat it.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register for and use Nestflow, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, password, and user profile information
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued identification documents (when required for verification)
  • Professional Information: Professional licenses, certifications, business details, professional qualifications (for users registering as professionals)
  • Listing Information: Details about properties, businesses, services, events, travel accommodations, or items you list on our Platform
  • Communication Data: Messages exchanged with other users through our Platform
  • Payment Information: Information necessary to process platform fees and payments (processed through Stripe)
  • Media Content: Photos, videos, and other media you upload to the Platform

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access and use our Platform, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our Platform, including pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Location Data: General location information based on IP address or more precise location when permitted by you
  • Log Data: Server logs, IP addresses, timestamps, and referring/exit pages

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Provide, improve, and develop our Platform services
  • Verify user identity and professional credentials
  • Process transactions and platform fees
  • Display your listings to potential interested parties
  • Facilitate connections between users
  • Send notifications about activity relevant to you
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure compliance with our terms and policies
  • Detect and prevent fraud or other harmful activities
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

3.1 With Other Users

  • When you create listings, certain information will be visible to other users
  • When you engage with listings or services offered by other users
  • When you participate in community forums or discussions

3.2 With Service Providers

  • Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf
  • Payment processors, specifically Stripe, for handling payments and platform fees
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Analytics providers

3.3 For Legal Reasons

  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
  • To enforce our terms of service
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Nestflow, our users, or others

3.4 Business Transfers

  • In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition

4. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no electronic transmission or storage technology is completely secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. YOUR PRIVACY CHOICES

5.1 Account Information

You can review, update, or delete your account information by accessing your account settings on our Platform.

5.2 Communication Preferences

You can opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the instructions in these communications.

5.3 Location Information

You can control location permissions through your device settings.

5.4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. CATEGORY-SPECIFIC PRIVACY CONSIDERATIONS

6.1 Residential & Commercial Properties

  • Listing information is displayed publicly but contact details are only shared with interested parties
  • Property location data is displayed according to user preferences

6.2 Businesses For Sale

  • Business information is displayed publicly but sensitive financial information is only shared with verified interested parties

6.3 Connections, Loans & Insurance

  • Professional verification data is collected but underlying documentation is only retained for verification purposes
  • License information is displayed publicly to establish credibility

6.4 Travel & Events

  • Booking information is shared only with relevant service providers
  • Location data for venues and activities is displayed publicly

6.5 Community

  • User-generated content in forums and marketplace listings is publicly viewable
  • Contact information is only shared between consenting parties

6.6 Media

  • Uploaded content is publicly viewable according to your sharing preferences
  • Usage metrics on content engagement are collected in aggregate form

6.7 Jobs

  • Resume information is only shared with employers you apply to
  • Professional qualifications may be verified with relevant authorities

6.8 Purchase

  • High-value item listing information is publicly displayed but transaction details remain private
  • Verification processes for high-value transactions include additional identity checks

7. PAYMENT PROCESSING

7.1 Stripe Integration

We use Stripe as our payment processing partner. When you make payments through our Platform:

  • Payment card information is collected directly by Stripe and not stored on our servers
  • Stripe may collect your name, email address, billing address, payment card information, and transaction details
  • Your payment information is handled according to Stripe's Privacy Policy (available at https://stripe.com/privacy)
  • We receive limited information from Stripe to confirm successful transactions, such as the last four digits of your card, transaction IDs, and payment status

7.2 Platform Fees

For certain services on our Platform, such as charter bookings, we charge platform fees that are processed through Stripe. The payment processing for these fees follows the same privacy standards outlined above.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Platform is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the country in which you reside. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using our Platform, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

10. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy frequently to stay informed about how we protect your information.

12. USER VERIFICATION POLICY

To maintain the integrity and security of our Platform, we verify the identity of users in certain circumstances:

Verification documents are securely stored and handled in compliance with applicable data protection regulations.
